Travel & Estate Logistics

Transparent answers to high-end travel concerns. Resolve airline, speedboat, beach path, and staffing logistics seamlessly.

For private aviation, Clayton J. Lloyd International Airport (AXA) in Anguilla has a 5,400-foot runway accommodating large luxury turboprops and mid-size private jets. For commercial flights, most guests fly directly into St. Maarten (SXM). Upon arrival at SXM, our private concierge will greet you, assist with luggage, and transfer you to a private speed-charter boat that brings you directly to Anguilla in 20 minutes. A private luxury SUV will be waiting at the dock to escort you directly to the villa.

Songbird Villa sits on an elevated coastal cliff to capture sunset views and sea breezes. The estate features a beautifully manicured, private sandy footpath leading directly down the cliff face onto the pristine white sands of Rendezvous Bay Beach. The walk is extremely gentle and takes less than five minutes, allowing you to transition from the infinity pool deck to the beach without stepping on public roads.

Standard rentals include daily housekeeping, laundry/laundress services, dedicated groundskeepers, and our professional Estate Manager who acts as your personal concierge. We handle daily maintenance, pool cleaning, beach towels setup, and local reservations. Luxury add-on services—such as a Michelin-trained private chef, personal 24/7 butler, wellness spa therapist, and private security—can be dynamically arranged by our team at the time of booking.
